Who is Mukunth Arjunan?

Mukunth Arjunan is a data scientist from LA but now based in New York City.   • Best Buy
  • Wawa
  • Victoria's Secret
  • Verizon

In his free time, he likes to swim, paint, walk the dogs and spend time in nature or with friends. He is always open to book recommendations and jobs!
